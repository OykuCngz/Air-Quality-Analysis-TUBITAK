ML Architecture & Data Fusion

1. Bidirectional Data Hub (160,107 Rows) Combines Istanbul Kandilli baseline with Seoul's large-scale gas distributions for extreme-case transfer learning.
2. Residual Denoising (Random Forest) Initial model filters out top 50% environmental noise. Final model is trained purely on the cleaned data manifold yielding 0.87 R² accuracy.
3. Explainable AI (SHAP TreeExplainer) Black-box decision logic is disassembled in real-time, isolating individual meteorological features (Temp, Hum, Gases) influencing the PM predictions.

TÜBİTAK 2209-A Documentation

AeroPredict ML is an advanced Air Quality inference engine designed as a completely software-defined Digital Twin alternative to high-cost IoT sensor networks.

  • Spatial Solution: By leveraging Open-Meteo Satellite feeds, spatial constraints of placing physical devices are eliminated.
  • Accuracy Solution: Machine Learning overrides standard low-cost sensor drift, pushing accuracy metrics up to 87%.
  • Policy Ready: Integrated PDF reporting pipeline allows direct exportation of actionable environmental data to policymakers.
